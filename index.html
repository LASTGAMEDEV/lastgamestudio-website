THE VISION

Real Agriculture. Real Science. Real Game.

We're not making another cartoon farm simulator. We're building a game where every decision matters — soil pH, crop rotation, weather patterns, market prices. If you studied agriculture, you'll feel at home. If you didn't, you'll learn the real deal.

This is a passion project born from real agricultural engineering knowledge. Every tractor model is researched. Every crop growth cycle is accurate. This is the farming game for people who take farming seriously.

  • Research-based crop and soil simulation
  • Licensed and modeled real-world machinery
  • Dynamic weather and market systems
  • No pay-to-win, no fake timers

GAME FEATURES

What Makes This Different

Every feature is built around one question: does this feel real?

🌱

Soil Science Engine

Real pH levels, nitrogen cycles, moisture retention. Your soil remembers what you planted last season. Ignore it and watch your yields crash.

🚜

Licensed Machinery

John Deere, Case IH, Fendt — researched and modeled from real specs. Horsepower, fuel consumption, maintenance schedules. Your garage matters.

🌦️

Living Weather

Micro-climates, seasonal shifts, unexpected storms. Real historical weather data from regions around the world. Adapt or go bankrupt.

📈

Live Market

Crop prices fluctuate based on global supply, demand, and events. Sell at the right time or watch your profits rot in the silo.

🏗️

Build Your Farm

Barns, silos, irrigation systems, greenhouses. Every building serves a purpose and costs real money to maintain. Plan your expansion carefully.

👥

Community Driven

Backers at $12+ get voting power. You propose features, the community votes, we build what you actually want. This is your game too.

Done
